About Me

I am a Software Engineer and Senior Manager with 20+ years of experience in research, software development, technical leadership, and people management. At IBM, I led the SilverGate Research Team in Argentina for nearly a decade, managing a multidisciplinary group of more than 20 Software Engineers, Researchers, PhDs, and technical leaders distributed across Argentina and the U.S.

My experience spans security research, access control systems, cryptography, AI foundations, and large-scale software engineering. I am a member of the IBM Academy of Technology and an active core leader in IBM Technical Council Argentina, with a strong focus on Women in Technology initiatives.

Outside of my technical career, I am passionate about mentoring, social impact, photography, and pilates.
